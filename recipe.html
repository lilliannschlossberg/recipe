Recipe

Ingredients

  • Fresh or frozen fruit
  • La croix SParkling Water
  • Popsicle Mold

Steps

  • Add a couple drops of Stevia to the LaCroix and mix together
  • Insert fruit into the popsicle or push pop molds.
  • Pour liquid over the fruit and close the mold.
  • Allow it to freeze for a few hours.
  • Take out and enjoy!
